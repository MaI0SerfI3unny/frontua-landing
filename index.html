OUR MISSION

The mission of our non-governmental organization is to save as many lives as possible in this war.
Therefore, our team has joined forces, put all our efforts and knowledge to bring Ukraine closer to victory.
Since 2014, part of our team has been creating the non-governmental organization "Aerorozvidka" and building reconnaissance drones for the army. Now we focus on civil- ian security in the hottest of battlefields.
In collaboration with the non-governmental organization "Digital Transformation Institute", we have all the necessary approvals to officially procure, build and transfer the drones to the war zone.

WHY DRONES?

Drones save lives!

Our drones save hundreds of lives daily thanks to their small mass, day, and night long-range vision. In addition, aerial reconnaissance allows the military to analyze the operational situation from the sky, ensuring the timely movement of positions and avoiding combat attacks and collisions. It is critical in densely populated areas.
Mobile aerial reconnaissance provides timely and accurate shelter, safe evacuation for civilians and saves hundreds of lives of children, women, and elderly daily.

WHAT ARE WE DOING?

We are raising funds to construct and purchase drones capable of providing aerial reconnaissance.
It allows establishing safe routes for the evacuation of populations from combat zones.

ABOUT THE FOUNDER


Dmytro Lisenbart has a creative background in animation and production having run a successful animation studio in Kyiv since 2005. Dmytro joined the volunteer movement following the outbreak of war in Eastern Ukraine in 2014. He co-founded the NGO Aerorozvidka (Aerial Reconnaissance), which later evolved into a listed company.

Aerorozvidka has worked closely with the Ukrainian armed forces throughout eight-year conflict, helping the military to provide more effective defences to protect civilians. Dmytro and his team have extensive experience in funding and implementing unmanned aircraft systems.

Following the Russian invasion on February 24th, Dmytro established a new organisation Front UA to supply the armed forces with specialised drones designed to assist humanitarian efforts on the front line.
